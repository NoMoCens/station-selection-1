1. Click on the icon of the ad blocker extension on your browser.
You can usually find this icon in the upper right corner of your screen.
You may have more than one ad blocker installed.
2. Within the pop-up menu open the ad blocker’s settings page.
The settings page can usually be opened by clicking a cogwheel icon or three dots.
3. Follow the instructions for adding a specific website - gamedistribution.com - to your Whitelist
or Allowlist.
You may have to select the appropriate menu option, and type gamedistribution.com when
asked which website you would like to allow ads from.
4. Save the changes you have made and close your ad blocker’s settings tab.
You may need to refresh the page you are visiting for the change to take effect.
If you are experiencing any difficulty disabling your ad blocker, please contact our support team.
