AdBlock
1. In the upper right corner of your browser toolbar, click on the AdBlock icon.
2. Click on the cogwheel icon in the upper right corner of the pop-up menu.
The AdBlock Settings will now open in a new tab.
3. Select Customize in the menu to the left.
4. Click on the Edit button next to the heading. Manually edit your filters.
5. Paste the following line into the input field:
@@||gamedistribution.com/*^$document
6. Click on the Save button.
