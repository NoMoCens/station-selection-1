AdBlock Plus
1. In the upper right corner of your browser toolbar, click on the AdBlock Plus icon.
2. Click on the cogwheel icon in the upper right corner of the pop-up menu.
The AdBlock Plus Settings will now open in a new tab.
3. Click on Allowlisted websites.
4. Enter the website gamedistribution.com and click Add website.
5. Close the AdBlock Plus Settings tab.
